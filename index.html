Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How often should I service my AC unit?

We recommend servicing your AC unit at least twice a year - once before the cooling season begins and once before the heating season starts. Regular maintenance ensures optimal performance, extends the lifespan of your system, and can prevent costly repairs.

What size AC unit do I need for my home?

The size of the AC unit needed depends on several factors including your home's square footage, ceiling height, insulation quality, number of windows, and local climate. As a general rule, you need about 20 BTUs per square foot. Our technicians can perform a proper load calculation to determine the exact size required for your space.

How long does an AC installation take?

A typical AC installation takes about 4-8 hours for a residential system, depending on the complexity of the installation and whether ductwork modifications are needed. Commercial installations may take longer. We always strive to complete installations efficiently while ensuring quality work.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help make your HVAC investment more affordable. We work with several financial institutions to provide competitive rates and payment plans tailored to your budget. Contact us for more details about our current financing options.

What areas do you service in Florida?

We service from West Palm Beach to Homestead, Florida. Our service area includes Boca Raton, Fort Lauderdale, Miami, and all surrounding communities. We also offer 24/7 emergency services throughout our service area.
